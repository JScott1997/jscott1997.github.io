About Me

Hi there, my name is Jared Scott. I am a recent game programming graduate. Ever since my Dad let me play his NES at age 4, I have created some many fantastic memories playing video games. I want to be a part of creating something that will form those memories for other people.

During my years at school I have gained programming experience in animation, gameplay, UI, networking, graphics and more. Participating in two capstone projects allowed me to apply and develop my skills in many areas of game programming. These projects most importantly taught me how great game development is when you are a part of a passionate and focused team. I am eager to bring my problem solving skills and strong work ethic into the game industry.
